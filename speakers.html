Dr. Sarah Chen

AI Research Director, TechCorp

Talk: "The Future of Neural Networks: Beyond Current Limitations"

Bio: Dr. Sarah Chen is a leading expert in neural networks and machine learning with over 15 years of experience in AI development. She holds a PhD in Computer Science from MIT and has published over 50 research papers in top-tier conferences.

Her work focuses on developing more efficient and interpretable neural network architectures. She has led teams that created breakthrough AI systems used by millions of people worldwide.

Achievements:

  • Winner of the AI Innovation Award 2023
  • Featured in Forbes "Top 40 Under 40 in Tech"
  • Author of "Neural Networks Unleashed" (bestseller)

Marcus Rodriguez

SpaceX Engineering Lead

Talk: "Mars Mission Technology: Engineering the Journey to Another Planet"

Bio: Marcus Rodriguez is a pioneering aerospace engineer working on next-generation rocket propulsion systems and Mars mission technology at SpaceX. With a Master's in Aerospace Engineering from Caltech, he has been instrumental in developing the technologies that will enable human settlement on Mars.

He leads the team responsible for developing life support systems and habitat technologies for long-duration space missions.

Achievements:

  • Lead engineer on 12 successful SpaceX missions
  • Holds 15 patents in aerospace technology
  • NASA Exceptional Engineering Achievement Medal recipient
